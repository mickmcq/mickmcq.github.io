About

UX Studio is a week-long design jam hosted by the iSchool in the Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences. From January 25th-29th, 2021, teams of four will work together virtually to create designs for various non-profit organizations.

We'll meet on Zoom at 5PM on January 25th to kick off the event and get the charges from the non-profits.

We'll meet again on Zoom at 5PM on January 29th for presentations and judging.

FAQ

Where can I give feedback about the event? Please take the survey

What will the Monday and Friday events be like?The agenda for both events is available. The zoom link for the Friday presentation is 99188354170 with a password of 14623 (our zipcode).

What will the week look like? Good question! UX Club officers have put together a checklist you can use to pace yourself.

Do I need a team to register? - No, teams will be assigned later on. You'll be assigned to a team of students in different years of their program, e.g., one freshman, one sophomore, one junior, and one senior on a team.

What non-profits will be participating? - Catholic Family Center, Water for South Sudan, and Rochester Museum and Science Center have all agreed to participate. So there are three groups from which you may choose to do your design.

Can any major participate? - Yes! The competition is open to all RIT students!

Will there be prizes? - Each member of the three winning teams (one per non-profit) will receive a trophy!

Who is judging the competition? - The judging panel includes the Dean of the College, the Director of the iSchool, one iSchool professor, and one representative from each of the non-profits.
