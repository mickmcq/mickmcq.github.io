About

UX Studio was a week-long design jam hosted by the iSchool in the Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences. From January 25th-29th, 2021, teams of four worked together virtually to create designs for various non-profit organizations.

We met on Zoom at 5PM on January 25th to kick off the event and get the charges from the non-profits.

We met again on Zoom at 5PM on January 29th for presentations and judging.
